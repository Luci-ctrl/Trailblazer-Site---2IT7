The City College of Calamba has three main buildings -- Admin Building, Rizal Building, and JMC Building -- where students are taught and trained to be a global professional. CCC dedicates these buildings to their cherished scholars who are the Nation's hope! These buildings are full of love, joy, and hope.

ADMIN BUILDING

The Admin Building serves as the central hub for the institution’s administrative operations. It houses key personnel and services responsible for running the school smoothly and supporting both students and staff.

RIZAL BUILDING

A school building named after Jose Rizal—the national hero of the Philippines—carries profound symbolic meaning. Rizal, known for his advocacy for education, reform, and freedom, embodies values that are crucial in any academic institution.

JMC BUILDING

A school building named after a former mayor reflects more than just a gesture of recognition—it conveys appreciation for local leadership and serves as a lasting symbol of civic contributions.

About Us

Welcome to Trailblazer, your go-to guide for navigating our school campus with ease. Whether you're a student attending class, a teacher heading to your next lecture, or a visitor trying to find a specific room, we're here to help you get there quickly and without confusion.

This Team is composed of City College of Calamba Students dedicated to make a simple navigating system in order to help new students navigate and locate their desired classrooms. The site was made as a partial fulfillment for the course Web Design and Programming.

OUR MISSION

To provide a user-friendly platform that helps students, faculty, teachers, visitors, and parents easily navigate school campuses by offering accurate, real-time guidance to their desired classrooms. We strive to reduce confusion and time spent searching for locations, enhancing the overall school experience for all users. Through this service, we aim to support academic success, improve campus efficiency, and foster a welcoming environment for everyone.

OUR VISION

Be the leading digital tool for school navigation, offering a comprehensive and accessible solution that meets the needs of all campus visitors. We aspire to create a seamless experience where students, faculty, and visitors can confidently find their way around, reducing stress and improving overall satisfaction. Ultimately, we aim to contribute to a more organized and efficient school environment that prioritizes ease of use and inclusivity for all.
